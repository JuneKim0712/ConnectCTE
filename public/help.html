Search, Filter, and Sort

To search, click on search bar and type the information you want to search.

To filter the information, press "Filter By Type" or/and "Filter By Sector" and select the catergory you want to filter.

To sort the table, press the index of the table (e.g. name, type, sector) and the table with sort from ascending order of the index, press again to make the table sort in descending order.

Find the button with that says “reset” and click it to reset the filters you set on the list.

To add a business partner, press "Add Partner" and manually type in the information for a business partner.

To import the data, press the import button and choose the csv file you want to input and press submit to import the data. To export the data, press the export button to download a csv file of the partner's data.

Data Security

To logout of your account, click the logout button in upper right corner next to your name.

Moderators can add, edit, delete the business partners' data. Super moderators can also add, edit, and delete the business partners' data as well as edit the of permission of the moderators.

The database is in Google's data storage, one of most invulnerable database and we set the database to take backups every day.
