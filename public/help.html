Frequently asked Q&A

If you are a moderator, find the blue button that says “add partner” and click it. There you can type the information needed like the name of the business, their contact information, and more!

Click on the column like “name” or “type” to choose how the partners will be sorted based on the particular detail clicked. Clicking it again will change the sorting from ascending to descending

Find the search bar on the front page with the word “Search…” in the text box. Type any letters or numbers you would like to filter from the business and community partners list. You don't need to hit enter as it is live search!

Find the drop-down menu near the top of the front page with the word “type” to filter by type or “section” to filter by section. From there, click on one of the types to filter by or sections to filter by.

Find the blue button with that says “reset” and click it to reset the filters you set on the list.
